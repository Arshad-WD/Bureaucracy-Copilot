# Business Requirements Document (BRD)

# Project Name

Bureaucracy Copilot

(MVP: Scholarship Copilot)

Version 1.0

---

# Executive Summary

Bureaucracy Copilot is an AI-powered citizen assistance platform designed to simplify access to scholarships, government schemes, subsidies, and public benefits.

The initial version, Scholarship Copilot, focuses on helping students discover scholarships they are likely eligible for based on their academic, demographic, and financial profiles.

The platform addresses a major problem: millions of students miss scholarship opportunities because information is fragmented, difficult to understand, and spread across multiple government and private portals.

By providing personalized recommendations, eligibility assessments, document guidance, and deadline reminders, Bureaucracy Copilot aims to increase access to educational funding and reduce information barriers.

---

# Business Problem

Students face several challenges when searching for scholarships:

* Lack of awareness about available opportunities.
* Difficulty understanding eligibility criteria.
* Multiple websites with inconsistent information.
* Missed application deadlines.
* Confusing documentation requirements.
* No centralized discovery platform.

As a result:

* Eligible students fail to apply.
* Scholarships remain underutilized.
* Educational opportunities are lost.

# Business Rules

## Rule 1

The platform must never guarantee scholarship approval.

---

## Rule 2

The platform must always provide official scholarship sources.

---

## Rule 3

AI responses must be based on verified scholarship information.

---

## Rule 4

Expired scholarships must not appear in active recommendations.

---

## Rule 5

Users must be informed that final eligibility decisions rest with scholarship providers.
